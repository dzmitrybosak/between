Between

Support

We are here to help.

If something in Between is not working as expected, or you have a question about the app, email us at between.official.app@gmail.com.

Before you write

Please do not include the text of your situation, your answers, or other sensitive personal information. A short description of the issue, your device model, iOS version, and the app version are usually enough to help.

Subscriptions

To manage or cancel an App Store subscription, open your Apple Account subscription settings. In Between, you can also use Restore Purchases on the Unlimited screen if a previous purchase is not showing.

Apple handles payments, refunds, and billing. For help with a charge or refund, contact Apple Support.

Privacy requests

For questions about privacy or requests related to your data, email between.official.app@gmail.com. See our Privacy Policy for information about how Between handles data.

Important

Between is a reflection aid, not an emergency or crisis service. If you or someone else may be in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional.
